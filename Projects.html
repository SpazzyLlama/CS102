Project Space Animal: Galactic Adventures

Concept: A charming 3D adventure game blending the exploration of *Zelda* with the cozy customization of *Animal Crossing* and the innovative movement of *Mario Galaxy*. Players take on the role of a space-faring animal tasked with restoring balance to a constellation of unique planets.

  • Key Features: Open-world exploration, crafting, character customization, puzzle-solving, narrative-driven quests.
  • My Role: Game design, level layout, initial prototyping, narrative development.
  • Technologies: Unity Engine, C# (for scripting), Blender (for asset concepts).

Unity Roll-a-Ball: First Steps in Game Dev

Overview: My introductory project to the Unity development environment. This simple game involves navigating a sphere through a maze to collect various pick-ups, demonstrating fundamental game development concepts.

  • Lessons Learned: Basic physics, input handling, scene management, UI elements, collision detection, object instantiation and destruction.
  • My Contribution: Full implementation following tutorial, with minor custom aesthetic and gameplay tweaks.
  • Technologies: Unity Engine, C#.

Overwatch Board Game: Tactical Tabletop Fun

Design Challenge: To adapt the fast-paced, hero-shooter mechanics of *Overwatch* into a physical board game format, moving away from traditional dice rolls towards strategic ability usage and objective control.

  • Unique Mechanics: Card-driven actions, ultimate abilities, team objective zones, unique hero movement patterns.
  • My Focus: Ruleset design, balancing hero abilities, prototype creation, playtesting and iteration.
  • Tools: Physical prototypes, Adobe InDesign (for card layouts), extensive playtesting sessions.
